Respect Everyone

Be polite and considerate to all members, regardless of language or background.

No Hate Speech or Discrimination

Any form of racism, sexism, or offensive remarks is strictly prohibited.

Keep Discussions Friendly

Avoid arguments, personal attacks, and sensitive topics like politics or religion.

No Spamming

Do not flood the chat with excessive messages, links, or promotions.

Be Patient with Language Barriers

Respect that not everyone speaks the same language fluently.

No Offensive or Inappropriate Content

Avoid sharing explicit, violent, or disturbing material.

Report Issues Privately

If you see a rule violation, report it to the admins instead of causing public disputes.
